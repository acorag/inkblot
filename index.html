What happened to Inky? Bro, crazy story! Okay, so, like, I guess the construction was totally finished and the boss came in and was like "I don't like any of it, tear it all down and start over!"

I know, right? The workers were totally steamed because they worked really hard to make everything perfect and stuff. So all at once, all the workers started to riot. They destroyed everything. I got really scared and I locked myself in the library. Kinda lucky I guess, because that was, like, the only room with any content in it.

So I guess I kinda saved the day. Then the rioting stopped as suddenly as it started. It went from a deafening roar one second, to crickets the next. After it went silent, I waited for a few minutes and when I looked outside they were all gone. I guess most of them just, like, walked out.

Yea, just like that. They all just left. Crazy, right? The entire place was completely destroyed. There was nothing left except the empty bookshelves in the library and I found a paintbrush, but everything else - gone.

So the boss found out and I thought for sure he would get, like, totally ticked, but he didn't. He walked in like nothing happened and when he saw me - he shrugged.

Then all of a sudden, the boss just started doing all the repairs by himself. He was working like a madman. I've never seen anything like it. He built that menu up there with all kinds of, like, mechanical gizmo things and he rebuilt all the pages with some flexible rubbery stuff. Yep, and he painted the entire place pink. So random, right?

You won't hear me complaining dude, I feel right at home now. If work gets too crazy, I just like, fade into the background. It's pretty far out, man.

Dude! This was mostly the boss, but I totally helped too. I painted the website name on the banner up there. It looks pretty awesome.
